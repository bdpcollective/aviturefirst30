Aviture First 30

Built for On-boarding New Hires

By Brian Pope
  • How it Works Welcome to Aviture! On your first day you will be given a set of tasks to help get acclimated with Aviture. Complete as many tasks as possible within the first 30 days of your start date. Need help? Talking to your Technical lead, coach and other Aviturians is recommended.
  • Task Categories

    Required

    Required tasks are designed to get you familiar with things are manidtory and will be required to do on a regular basis. These tasks are not optional. Examples: Basecamp Posts, ADP and Timesheets.

    Documentation & Training

    Documentation & Training tasks are designed to get you familiar with Aviture systems and applications. Examples: Aviture Cloud, Slack and the Aviture blog.

    Culture & Values

    The Culture & Values tasks are developed to get you involved with different groups and activites that Aviture thrives around. Examples: Making coffee, Book Club and the Aviture Connect Slack Channel.

    Socialization

    Socialization task were created to get to know other Aviturians. Examples: Meeting a remote Aviturian, go to an Aviture Happy Hour and going out to lunch with other Aviturians.

  • Vision Create a better way for new employees to become acclimated into Aviture's culture, systems and initiatives.
  • Goal Build an interactive system to onboard new employees to fit in our culture.
  • Priciples Interactive The Aviture 30 pairs learning with hands on activities. New Hires move around the office, meet new people, and understand who Aviture is as a company by experiencing different activities. Self Sustainable This on-boarding system is flexible to match Avitureâ€™s evolving culture and benefits. Tasks should be able to be added and removed when appropriate. This system should not impact the new employees schedule or strain an existing employees schedule. The Aviture 30 works with minimal maintenance. Accesible The Aviture first 30 should be easy to access. Tasks and completion sign-off should be accessible with minimal effort to allow a new hire as frictionless onboarding as possible. Fairness All tasks must be available during the new hires first 30 days. Tasks that are seasonal will not be added. Tasks must fit Avitures current culture and will be curated to match over time.
