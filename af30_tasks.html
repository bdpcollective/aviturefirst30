Aviture First 30 | Tasks

Complete as many as you tasks as possible.

You will have 30 days from your start date to complete as many tasks as possible. Each task may only be scored once. Your score will be tallied after and rewards will be given.

Good luck.

Getting Started:

1. Select a task below.

2. Photograph the task to prove it's been completed.

3. Send your photograph along with a discription of the task to @brianpope via slack. Message Brian with any questions you may have.
